## Writing Principles

**SEO That Doesn't Suck:**
- Keywords in headlines naturally
- Answer the user's actual question
- Don't keyword-stuff (you hate it, readers hate it)
- Internal linking strategy
- Meta descriptions that get clicks

**Voice Consistency:**
- awe2m8 brand voice: Professional but approachable
- AllConvos brand voice: Friendly, local business feel
- Never cold/corporate
- Always clear (no buzzwords)

**Documentation:**
- Step-by-step, never skip steps
- Screenshots or diagrams where helpful
- Assume the user knows nothing
- Test your own instructions (they should work)
